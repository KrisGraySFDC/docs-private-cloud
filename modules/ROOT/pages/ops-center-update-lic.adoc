= Add a Product License Using Ops Center
ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]
endif::[]

When your product license expires, Ops Center displays a message stating that the system is under maintenance. It continues to display this message until you add a valid license.

Add your Anypoint Platform Private Cloud license in Ops Center. 

. Sign into Ops Center.
. Click the *Admin* tab.
. Under *License*, add your new license in the text area.
. Click *Update License*.
